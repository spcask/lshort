Special characters:
\# \$ \% \^{} \& \_ \{ \} \~{} \textbackslash

\^{} and \^\ look the same.

\~{} and \~\ look the same.

\^foo shows the circumflex accent.

\~foo shows the tilde diacritic.

breaking.\\
